## What is LionBot?

LionBot is a free Discord bot built for productivity. It tracks study and work time in voice channels, manages tasks and reminders, runs a coin economy with leaderboards, and gives your server tools to stay motivated.

## Adding LionBot to Your Server

Getting started takes less than a minute.

<InviteCTA text="Add LionBot to your server" compact />

Once invited, LionBot is ready to use immediately. No additional setup is required for basic features.

## Your First Commands

<CommandShowcase command="/help" description="See all available commands grouped by category." />

<CommandShowcase command="/my timezone" description="Set your timezone so stats, reminders, and sessions use the correct clock." />

<Tip>Not sure what your timezone is called? Start typing your city name and LionBot will autocomplete it for you.</Tip>

## What's Next?

Now that LionBot is in your server, explore these features:

- **Study Tracking** — Join a voice channel and LionBot automatically tracks your time
- **Tasks** — Create a personal to-do list with `/task create`
- **Pomodoro Timers** — Set up group study sessions with `/pomodoro create`
- **Economy** — Members earn coins for activity, spend them in the server shop
